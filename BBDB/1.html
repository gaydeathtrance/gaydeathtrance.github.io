by Big Bruiser Dope Boy

gay white men talking in black women voices to each other
in dark red bars with no black women around

I’ll show you dark red
here is a burgundy Nissan Maxima

with two 6-inch subs in the back
dicks on all your cheeks

that’s rouge, and this is murder
in the dark bars I imagine your faces as skulls

skulls going to the bathroom to do coke
faces of the dead in a red and black stream

your kinks aren’t kinks
The Kinks are kinkier than you

I don’t want to talk to any of you
and when you talk to me, I look somewhere else and imagine death
