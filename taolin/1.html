Gay Death Trance About

Two Poems

by Tao Lin

Fish



Unable to open or close
my eyes opening doors
off the corridor of time

Gecko on the printer
pinging off the ionosphere
light encircles Earth

7.5 times a second
thought-behavior-feeling
complexes oscillate

Making my sense of smell
keener yet more tolerant
Convincing people

the invisible matters
Feeding parents fish oil
This and the third poem

reminded you of pictures
From far away
is anything a unity





Earthlight



Closed-eyed pinging
off the corridor of time.
Oscillating fish poems.
Invisible fish oil.
Pictures of far away.
